## THE ADJACENT ANGLE — AND WHY IT'S NOT ENOUGH

QuotaPath is a sales commission management platform — which is compensation-adjacent to payroll. It calculates what salespeople earn, integrates with CRM and financial data, and feeds outputs to payroll systems. This is the closest transferable angle:

- Commission management is "pre-payroll" — comp plans feed into payroll runs
- QuotaPath integrates with Rippling, one of the listed payroll vendors
- Financial platform integrations (QuickBooks, Stripe) overlap with payroll data flows

However, the JD's hard requirement is **3+ years operating within payroll platforms** (ADP, Rippling, Paychex, Gusto) as a practitioner — not integrating data into them from a commission tool. A CSA at HiBob needs to configure payroll runs, troubleshoot payroll processing issues, understand BenAdmin setup, and advise clients on US payroll compliance. That is a distinct domain that commission management doesn't substitute for.
